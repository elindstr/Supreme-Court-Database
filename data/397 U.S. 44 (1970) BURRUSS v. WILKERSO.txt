U.S. Supreme Court  BURRUSS v. WILKERSON, 397 U.S. 44 (1970) 397 U.S. 44 BURRUSS ET AL. v. WILKERSON ET AL.  APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR  THE WESTERN DISTRICT OF VIRGINIA. No. 864. Decided February 24, 1970  Affirmed. Carl Rachlin, Melvin L. Wulf, and Eleanor Holmes Norton for appellants. Robert Y. Button, Attorney General of Virginia, Richard N. Harris, Assistant Attorney General, John S. Davenport III, and Henry T. Wickham for appellees. Briefs of amici curiae in support of appellants were filed by Ramsey Clark, David Rubin, and John W. Douglas for the National Education Association et al., and by Joseph L. Rauh, Jr., John Silard, J. Albert Woll, Thomas E. Harris, Stephen I. Schlossberg, and David A.
