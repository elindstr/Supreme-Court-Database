APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OHIO. No. 537. Decided January 19, 1959.  Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied. Reported below: 168 Ohio St. 65, 151 N.E.2d 223. Robert G. Day for appellant.
