U.S. Supreme Court  LONG v. PARKER, 384 U.S. 32 (1966) 384 U.S. 32 LONG v. PARKER, WARDEN. ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS FOR  THE THIRD CIRCUIT. No. 821, Misc. Decided April 18, 1966.  Certiorari granted; vacated and remanded. Petitioner pro se. Solicitor General Marshall for respondent.  PER CURIAM.  Upon consideration of the suggestion of the Solicitor General and an examination of all of the papers submitted, the motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Court of Appeals for further proceedings.
