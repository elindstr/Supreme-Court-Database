APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE EASTERN DISTRICT  OF PENNSYLVANIA. No. 484. Decided December 19, 1960.  Appeal dismissed. Reported below: 186 F. Supp. 776. George B.
