APPEAL FROM THE UNITED STATES DISTRICT COURT FOR THE EASTERN DISTRICT OF  PENNSYLVANIA. No. 297. Decided October 24, 1960.  Judgment vacated and case remanded. Reported below: 177 F. Supp. 398. C. Brewster Rhoads, Percival R. Rieder and Philip H. Ward III for appellants. Anne X. Alpern, Attorney General of Pennsylvania, and John D.
