U.S. Supreme Court  JOSEPH v. INDIANA, 359 U.S. 117 (1959) 359 U.S. 117 JOSEPH ET AL v. INDIANA.  CERTIORARI TO THE SUPREME COURT OF INDIANA.  No. 8. Argued January 19, 1959. Decided March 23, 1959.  Writ of certiorari dismissed as improvidently granted. Reported below: 236 Ind. 529, 141 N.E.2d 109. William S. Isham, acting under appointment by the Court, 355 U.S. 948, argued the cause and filed a brief for petitioners. Robert M. PER CURIAM.  The writ of certiorari is dismissed as improvidently granted.
