U.S. Supreme Court  PECK v. NEW YORK, 364 U.S. 662 (1961) 364 U.S. 662 PECK ET AL. v. NEW YORK.  APPEAL FROM THE COURT OF APPEALS OF NEW YORK.  No. 437. Decided January 16, 1961.  Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied. Reported below: 7 N.Y.2d 76, 163 N.E.2d 866. Kenneth W. Greenawalt and Harrop Freeman for appellants. Frank S. Hogan and Harold Roland Shapiro for appellee.
