ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF APPEALS  FOR THE SIXTH CIRCUIT. No. 95. Decided January 27, 1969.  Certiorari granted; 390 F.2d 205, vacated and remanded.
