ERROR TO THE

Syllabus

															A writ of error will not lie on a judgment of nonsuit.

															The Court directed the writ of error to be dismissed.

															Page 17 U. S. 74

															JUDGMENT. This cause came on to be heard, on the transcript of
the record, on consideration whereof it is adjudged and ordered
that the writ of error be, and the same is, hereby dismissed with
costs, the plaintiff having submitted to a nonsuit in the circuit
court.
