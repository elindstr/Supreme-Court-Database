ERROR TO THE CIRCUIT COURT

Syllabus 
Held that this Court will give time to procure affidavits as to the value of the matter in dispute. 
															Page 9 U. S. 288
															

															This being the last day of the term, and no affidavits having been produced, the writ of error was dismissed, this Court having no jurisdiction in the case.
