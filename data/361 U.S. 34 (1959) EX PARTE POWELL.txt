U.S. Supreme Court  EX PARTE POWELL, 361 U.S. 34 (1959) 361 U.S. 34 EX PARTE POWELL.  ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF  APPEALS FOR THE FIFTH CIRCUIT. No. 58, Misc. Decided October 19, 1959.  Certiorari granted; judgment vacated and case remanded to the District Court for a full hearing. Petitioner pro se. MacDonald Gallion, Attorney General of Alabama, and James W. Webb, Assistant Attorney General, for respondent.  PER CURIAM.  The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the District Court for a full hearing.
