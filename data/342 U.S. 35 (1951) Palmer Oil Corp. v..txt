APPEAL FROM THE SUPREME COURT OF OKLAHOMA
Syllabus 

															These causes are continued for such period as will enable appellants with all convenient speed to secure in an appropriate state proceeding a determination as to the effect on these appeals of the repeal of the state statute whose constitutionality is drawn in question by these appeals.

															The decision below is reported in 204 Okla. 543, 231 P.2d 997.

															PER CURIAM.

															The Court is advised that, on May 26, 1951, Laws 1951, p. 142, the Oklahoma Legislature repealed Okla.Stat. 1941, Cum.Supp. 1949, Tit. 52, §§ 286.1-286.17, the constitutionality of which is drawn in question by these appeals.  The causes are therefore ordered continued for such period as will enable appellants with all convenient speed to secure in an appropriate state proceeding a determination as to the effect of this repeal on the matters raised in these appeals.

															Causes continued.
