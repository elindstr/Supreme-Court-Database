Decided April 1, 1963.  Judgment vacated and case remanded to District Court with instructions to dismiss indictment. Reported below: 307 F.2d 585. Francis Heisler for petitioner. Solicitor General Cox, Assistant Attorney General Miller, Ralph S. Spritzer, Beatrice Rosenberg and Richard W. Schmude for the United States.  PER CURIAM.
