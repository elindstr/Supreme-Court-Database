U.S. Supreme Court  BONNIE v. GLADDEN, 391 U.S. 605 (1968) 391 U.S. 605 BONNIE v. GLADDEN, WARDEN.  ON PETITION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI TO THE UNITED STATES COURT OF  APPEALS FOR THE NINTH CIRCUIT. No. 731, Misc. Decided June 3, 1968.  Certiorari granted; 377 F.2d 555, vacated and remanded. Howard R. Lonergan for petitioner. Robert Y. Thornton, Attorney General of Oregon, and David H. Blunt, Assistant Attorney General, for respondent.  PER CURIAM.  The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit for further consideration in light of Carafas v. LaVallee, ante, p. 234.
