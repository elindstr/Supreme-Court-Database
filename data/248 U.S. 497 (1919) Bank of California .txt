															Decided on authority of Bank of California v. Richardson, ante, p.  248 U. S. 476.

															173 Cal. 398 reversed. 
															Page 248 U. S. 498
															

															PER CURIAM.

															This case is controlled by the opinion in Bank of California Richardson, ante, 248 U. S. 476.  Indeed, it was submitted without briefs upon the briefs filed in that case.  For the reasons stated in the previous case, therefore, the judgment here must be reversed and the case remanded for further proceedings not inconsistent with this opinion.
