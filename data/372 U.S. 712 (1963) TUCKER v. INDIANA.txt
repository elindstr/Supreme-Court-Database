Decided April 15, 1963.  Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; and case remanded. Petitioner pro se. Edwin K. Steers, Attorney General of Indiana, and Donald L. Adams, Deputy Attorney General, for respondent.  PER CURIAM.  The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded for further consideration in light of Lane v. Brown, 372 U.S. 477.
